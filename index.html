The Concept

Our goal is to create a visceral, fast-cut 30-second spot that takes Turtlebox from a trail to the bottom of a river, through a welding shop, to a bonfire, and proves the speaker never flinches.

This spot showcases Turtlebox's durability, battery life, and performance in the most challenging locations.

Every environment is deliberately extreme. Every shot is designed to make the viewer realize: the music is still going.



THE SHOT LIST

Scene by Scene

SCENE 01

RIVER CORRIDOR TRAIL

0:00 – 0:07

Opens wide on a cyclist with a Turtlebox mounted to the rear rack. Then falls off the bridge and into the water.

  • Wide tracking shot, cyclist on trail
  • Close-up mount detail, grille, logo
  • Low angle slow-mo, speaker tumbles toward river
  • Underwater splash cam, sinks with light refraction

SCENE 02

POOL PARTY

0:08 – 0:15

Arm plunges in, pulls the speaker out still playing. Three Turtleboxes pair up in Party Mode.

  • Arm reaches into the pool, pulls speaker from water
  • Close-up power button lights up, music resumes
  • Third speaker placed on grill grates
  • Phone screen showing three-speaker Bluetooth pairing
  • Wide handheld, crowd reacts to the volume

SCENE 03

WELDING SHOP

0:16 – 0:21

A welder works in a dimly lit garage. As sparks cascade directly onto the speaker grille, it sits completely unbothered.

  • Wide shot, welder at work, speaker in foreground
  • Close-up slow-mo, sparks rain onto the grille
  • Tight insert, logo visible through dying sparks

SCENE 04

BONFIRE

0:22 – 0:29

Truck tailgate drops. Speaker hits the ground facing a massive fire pit. Pallets go up. Gas pours. The fire erupts. The music doesn't stop.

  • Low angle wide, fire erupts, speaker silhouetted
  • Close-up grille glowing in firelight
  • Wide pull-back, group cheering, speaker front and center

END CARD

THE PARTNERSHIP

What We're Asking For

We'd love to partner with Turtlebox by featuring your speakers as hero products throughout the production. If possible, we'd appreciate receiving one unit of each speaker model for use during filming, though we completely understand availability may vary.

At minimum, the Original Gen 3 would play a key role in several planned underwater and durability-focused sequences central to the concept.

In exchange, ID43 Creative will produce and deliver a polished, broadcast-ready 30-second commercial spot showcasing the product in a cinematic, real-world environment.
